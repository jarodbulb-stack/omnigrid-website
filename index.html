About SOC

Social Omnigrid Connect (SOC) is a creator-focused toolset designed to help individuals and small businesses streamline their online workflow. SOC provides organizational tools, posting systems, and automation support for social media and affiliate marketers.

SOC does not generate content or publish automatically. Instead, SOC provides a structured workspace that helps users manage tasks, products, and posting schedules effectively.

Integrations & Supported Platforms

SOC connects with various third-party platforms such as:

  • TikTok Login Kit (for secure account authentication)
  • Facebook Pages (for manual posting preparation)
  • Affiliate marketplaces like Shopee, Lazada, and others

All platform connections require explicit user authorization. SOC complies with each platform’s policies.

Our Mission

We aim to make digital work easier for everyday creators—giving them tools to organize, prepare, and track their growth without technical complexity.
